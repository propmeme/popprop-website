PopProp Vision: Playfully Exploring Web3

PopProp is more than a game – it is the bridge between the familiar Web2 world and the limitless possibilities of Web3 on Solana. And the best part: it feels as easy as a Web2 game. You play, build and trade, while in the background the full power of Web3 comes alive.

The current demo illustrates how PopProp City could look in the future. It is the very beginning of a GameFi experience that will continue to grow – step by step from a simple demo into a vibrant, open world.

Every house in PopProp City will be playable – and each one is more than just pixels. It is a gateway to a real Web3 app on Solana. Wallets, tools, DeFi, games – as your city grows, so does your access to the entire ecosystem.

Thanks to the power of Solana, the bridge extends even further: Google Play and Apple App Store are no longer barriers – PopProp apps can seamlessly appear there, as well as in the Solana dApp Store. Web2 simplicity meets Web3 ownership.

In PopProp City you are not just a player – you are the master of your money, your property, and your decisions. Build your house, trade in the in-game stock exchange, exchange goods in multiplayer mode. NFTs will arrive – unique, tradable – and they will expand and shape the PopProp world.

PopProp makes the invisible visible: Web3 becomes tangible, playable, and fun. It is your key to enter a new world – free, open, community-driven – and as easy to access as any game you’ve ever played.
